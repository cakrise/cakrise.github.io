About Cakrise

Welcome to Cakrise! This is a fun, addictive, and simple game where your goal is to stack cake slices as high as you can.

This game was created by **Amit Soni** as a project to demonstrate the power of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for creating engaging web games.

How to Play

Simply tap on the screen to drop the moving cake slice. Try to align it perfectly with the slice below to get bonus points and build a stable tower. If you miss, the slice will be cut, making your tower smaller and harder to stack.
